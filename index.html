About the School

Brief History

+

Fortunato F. Halili National Agricultural School has been providing quality education since 1959. We are committed to nurturing future leaders and innovators in agriculture and technology.

Mission

+

To provide quality education that develops students' academic excellence, technical skills, and character formation, preparing them for higher education and productive careers.

Vision

+

To be a leading educational institution that produces globally competitive graduates who are equipped with knowledge, skills, and values for sustainable development.

Core Values

+

Excellence, Integrity, Service, Innovation, and Respect - these values guide our students and faculty in all endeavors.
